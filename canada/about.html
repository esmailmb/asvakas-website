Our Story

New Company, Decades of Know-How

Asvakas Inc. was founded on one premise: that clients deserve engineering talent without the overhead of large firms. Our leadership brings decades of experience across structural design, OBC compliance inspections, and temporary works — applied now through a lean, client-focused practice.

We serve property owners, contractors, developers, and municipalities from our Toronto office, delivering code-compliant engineering that stands the test of time. We are registered with Professional Engineers Ontario (PEO).
